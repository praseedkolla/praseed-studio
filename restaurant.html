Our Story

Crafted with fire,
served with soul

Ember & Oak began with a simple belief: the best meals are built around fire. Our open-flame kitchen brings you dishes that honour tradition while embracing modern technique.

Every ingredient is sourced from local farms. Every plate is a conversation between the chef and the season. We don't follow trends — we follow flavour.

"Fire doesn't just cook food — it transforms it."
